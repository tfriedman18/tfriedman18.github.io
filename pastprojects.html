This year for field academy I traveled to Laos and Cambodia with 14 other students. We were learning about meditation and the different cultures. We visited different temples, meditated with monks, visited Angkor Wat which is one of the 7 wonders of the world and visited many night markets. I found it really interesting how much trash was all over the place and how the communities and people seemed to be so far behind us with certain means of technology. We also learned a lot about the bombings in Laos and Cambodia and their impacts.

In 10th grade for m-term I interned today at the Long Island Aquarium. I was working with the aquarists and working with the different marine life. I fed the different fish and sharks and penguins. I also cleaned the cages of the the different marine life.

This summer I traveled to indonesia for a concervation project. I did research on the fish species in Raja Ampat. I was also working with the local children around the island.
